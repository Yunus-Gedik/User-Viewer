Prerequisites:
	- Swift Version 5
	- Deployment Target iOS 15
	- Connect to internet

How to run:
	- Open the "User Viewer.xcodeproj" file.
	- In Xcode, choose a simulator or connected physical device from the top-left dropdown.
	- Click the “Run” button in Xcode or press Cmd + R to build and launch the app.

How to run tests:
	- Follow first two steps of "How to run".
	- Press Cmd + U to run tests.
